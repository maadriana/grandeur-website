Grandeur International Marketing Corporation was established to provide an Integrated approach in the Distribution of products in the Philippines. More than 13 years experience of witnessing increasing complexities and unending opportunities in the Trade and Distribution (both Modern Trade and General Trade) brought us expertise in both sales and marketing - equally essential in the success of a product. Our experiences have shaped us to being prescient of clients' needs, and equipped us to tailor creative solutions.

The retail industry is recovering and growth rate is fast, benefiting from the omnichannel commerce observed globally. Grandeur International recognizes the mix of traditional and modern systems in the distribution landscape.

We aim to bridge gaps in the market through comprehensive data-driven research and development as well as its in-depth market studies. This outlook will help Trade, to carefully-select products with competitive pricing and proper placements in both modern and general trade markets. Through Sales-to-Trade data analysis, account universe penetration, optimal service levels, and B2B relationships, Grandeur International Marketing Corporation can ensure business growth and sustainability.
